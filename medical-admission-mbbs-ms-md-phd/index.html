Key points of Admission Process

Since the counselling for the deemed university is done by the MCC, the candidate do not need to fill a separate application form to initiate the admission process. The candidates will be required to register themselves in the website of MCC. On the basis of NEET score and the rank, the candidates will be invited to participate in the centralised counselling process.

Selection of Students

The selection of students shall be based solely on the merit of the candidates and for the determination of the merit the following criteria is adopted. 1.A candidate for admission to medical course must have obtained not less 50% marks in English and 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry and Biology taken together, both at Qualifying and competitive examinations. 2.Provided further that in respect of candidates belong to Scheduled castes/Scheduled Tribes and other backward classes ( OBC ) , the marks obtained in Physics, Chemistry and Biology taken together in qualifying examination be 40%

Deemed University MBBS 2023-24 Admission: Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility for admission to MBBS in Deemed Universities is similar to that of NEET 2022.Candidates who will be considered qualified for All India Quota seats on the basis of their rank in NEET.

Deemed University MBBS 2023-24 Admission: Essential Documents

At the time of admission or reporting at the allotted college, candidates will be required to furnish the following documents failing which candidates will not be allowed to take MBBS admission in Deemed University 2022.

  • Allotment letter
  • NEET 2022 Admit card
  • NEET 2022 Result
  • Date of Birth Certificate (if Matric Certificate does not bear the same)
  • Marksheet of Class 10 and 12
  • Twelve Passport size photographs (same as provided on the application form)
  • Provisional allotment letter issued online
  • A valid ID proof (Aadhar/ PAN/ Driving Licence/ Passport)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • PwD Certificate (if applicable)
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Study Certificate / Conduct Certificate / Character Certificate
  • 2 sets of xerox copy of all the documents
For Non Residential Indians (NRI) and Overseas Citizen of India (OCI) candidates following documents are required:
  • Passport copy of sponsorer
  • Sponsorship affidavit (stating that sponsor is ready to bear the expenses for the whole duration of study)
  • Relationship Affidavit (Relation of Candidate with the sponsor)
